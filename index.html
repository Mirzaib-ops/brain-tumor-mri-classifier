WHAT IT DOES

Built around the radiologist's worklist.

Every study gets a first pass before anyone opens it — so attention goes where it's needed first.

◎

Flag anomalies automatically

Each study is scanned for lesions, tumors, and structural irregularities as soon as it lands.

↑

HOW IT WORKS

From upload to sign-off.

A study moves through three stages — every one of them reviewable, and none of them final until you say so.

01

Upload the study

DICOM files arrive from your PACS automatically, or drag a study in directly.

02

Model reads the scan

The network segments the volume and flags regions of interest in under a minute.

03

You review and sign

Confirm, adjust, or dismiss each flag before it becomes part of the record.

DESIGNED FOR CLINICAL USE

Every flag is reviewable. Every read is logged.

✓

Nothing ships without a clinician. The model surfaces findings — it doesn't finalize a diagnosis.

✓

Full audit trail. Every flag, override, and sign-off is timestamped and attributable.

✓

Encrypted in transit and at rest. Studies stay inside your existing PACS environment.
